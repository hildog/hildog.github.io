WHO WE ARE

Hilary's Rad Eatery combines healthy and zaney to give you the most RAD food experience. Located in the heart of the Capitol Hill neighborhood in Seattle, we are dedicated to serving you unique dishes made from local ingredients. RAD VEGANS WELCOME!

ABOUT US

Hilary's Rad Eatery was founded in 2016 by Seattle Native Hilary Waite. Inspired by her quirkiness and search for affordable and healthy restaurant options, she opened Rad Eatery. Since it's opening, Hilary's Rad Eatery has inspired over 3,000 customers to eat healthy through it's unique and healful breakfast and lunch items.
